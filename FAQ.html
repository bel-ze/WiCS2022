Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What does Women Overcoming Adversity do?

Women Overcoming Adversity (WOA) is a platform built to connect and engage female-identifying people looking to get into or are already in the technology industry.
How do I join Women Overcoming Adversity?

The Women Overcoming Adversity (WOA) platform is free to join! You can look at previous posted threads, post anonymously, and listen into your favorite spaces.
What can I find on the website?

You can look at our section where some of the most popular posts of all times are posted. These include professionals describing their different career paths and what they do outside of technology.
Where can I find work opportunities?

Great question! We encourage users to post part-time and full-time opportunities and have started a where you can find internships, outside resources, and help for navigating the website.
